# CarbonScope 🌱  
**Agentic Net-Zero Intelligence**  
*Measure. Act. Restore. Together.*

🏆 **Runner-Up – MUJ HackX 2025**  
🎯 **Theme:** AI for Social Impact  

CarbonScope is a sustainability-focused web platform built during a hackathon to make **digital carbon emissions visible, measurable, and actionable**.

## 🌍 Problem Statement
Your daily digital life is not carbon-neutral — yet most digital emissions remain invisible.

- A single email can emit up to **4g of CO₂**
- **1 hour of HD video streaming** is equivalent to charging **20 phones**
- Cloud storage and data centers contribute nearly **3% of global emissions** (and rising)

When impact isn’t visible, behavior rarely changes.

## 💡 Our Solution
**CarbonScope makes carbon visible — and then enables action.**

It acts as a **carbon intelligence system** that:
- Tracks digital carbon emissions
- Provides meaningful sustainability insights
- Bridges digital activity with real-world restoration efforts

Think of CarbonScope as a **sustainability copilot**, not just a reporting dashboard.

## ⚙️ What CarbonScope Does
CarbonScope provides a dashboard that:
- Visualizes sustainability and environmental impact data
- Helps users interpret carbon-related insights
- Supports data-backed sustainability decisions
- Encourages measurable carbon reduction behavior

## 🌊 Restoration & Impact Vision
CarbonScope explores transparent and community-driven sustainability models:
- Verified restoration data uploads
- Participation from rural contributors and local communities
- Trust-based validation concepts for sustainability initiatives
- Focus on blue carbon ecosystems such as mangroves and coastal restoration

The platform is designed to be **accessible to individuals, NGOs, communities, and policymakers**, not just enterprises.
